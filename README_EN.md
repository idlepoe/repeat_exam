A drill-style study app for repeatedly reviewing written-exam questions for Korean **pastry** (제과) and **baking** (제빵) certification.

## Language

**This product is provided in Korean only.** The user interface, all questions and answers, explanations, and in-app text are in **Korean**. There is no English or other localized UI at this time.

## About the app

- Offers 40 exam sessions for pastry and 40 for baking (past released papers).
- Focuses on **review and memorization** with immediate correct answers shown—not on scoring mock attempts.
- Each item shows the correct choice right away, optimized for repeating question–answer pairs.
- Progress is saved so you can continue where you left off after closing the app.
- **No ads.**
- Layout and study flow are tuned for comfortable use on mobile.

## Who it’s for

- Anyone who wants to **drill past papers many times**, quickly.
- Anyone who learns well by pairing questions with answers and repeating them.
- Anyone who wants to study **without ads**.

## How to study

1. Choose **pastry** or **baking**.
2. Read each question while the correct answer is shown together.
3. Keep reviewing until question–answer pairs stick naturally.
